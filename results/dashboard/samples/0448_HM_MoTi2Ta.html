status_content
wrapped material in Ta foil as per 0447. As a result, no material visibly flew away. ~6% loss might be due to Ta foil on bottom not melting properly. there was also a black stain on the arc melted area (see ppt for picture) which may be carbon from the Ta foil. wiping Ta foil with ethanol before melting may help with this.
arc melted pellet did not split apart when hit with hammer.
pellet did not look crystalline and had no facets.

Screened for SC using dipstick on liquid He, resulting in <1% SC phase or none at all.

Calculation for ideal diamagnetic signature: -1503.34uV/cc sensitivity (calibrated by using lead pellet) * 0.054 cc (Sample volume, estimated by averaging densities and multiplied by sample mass) = -81uV
Measured signal: < 0.2uV, negative

Superconductivity: Not above 4.2 K based on dipstick test.
